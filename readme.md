# Dashboard Projects

A collection of business dashboards that show sales data and insights.

---

## Projects

### 1. Amazon Sales Dashboard
- Shows sales worldwide
- Total: 2.26M units sold
- Best customers and products tracked
- Tools: Power BI

### 2. BlinkIT Grocery Dashboard
- Tracks grocery store sales
- Total: $1.2M+ in sales
- Analyzes store sizes and product types
- Tools: Excel, Pivot Tables

### 3. Retail Intelligence Hub
- Shows global retail business
- Total: $13M in revenue
- Tracks profit, shipping, and customers
- Tools: Power BI
- Covers 139 countries
